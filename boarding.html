BOARDING

Our boarding facility can be your pet’s “home away from home”. Our well trained staff will provide your pet all the space and comfort your pet will need while you are away in our newly remodeled boarding facility. Since we have just 6 large fresh new runs, your “baby” will get the individual attention and love he/she deserves while here in our indoor air-conditioned boarding facility. The best part about boarding here at Stevens Animal Hospital is that if any issues arise while you are away, Dr. Mark and Dr. Chris are right here to make sure your companion gets the proper care right away.
