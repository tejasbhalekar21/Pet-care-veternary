Introduction

The organization has given dedicated and relentless service to all types of sick and injured animals. Approximately 9000 to 10000 animals are treated in a year out of which 300 to 400 are always in-patients. Our organization is registered with the Charity Commissioner; Registration No. F-304 (BOM) dated 2nd June 1953 under the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950.


During the last several years, the organization has tried their level best to render efficient services to sick and injured animals. Many new projects such as a Cardiac Centre, an Intensive Care Unit, an Animal Birth Control Centre, an Electric Crematorium and many more initiatives have been added. This has been made possible because of the generous support received from our well wishers / donors. The hospital constantly tries to provide better services to the animal world by incorporating new concepts of Animal Welfare and Animal Right. The hospital is constantly reducing cruelties to animals in the city of Bombay by employing SPCA Inspectors, social workers and by way of spreading education through awareness programmes.
